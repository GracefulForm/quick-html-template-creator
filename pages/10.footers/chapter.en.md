# Footers
The bottom of your website is a common place to put important information.

<div style="text-align:left"></div>
## Put It All Together
You can use the previous tools to build a footer.

**Example 1**
You can use a colored panel bar found in the Color Sections menu and add your copyright, business name, and links to your terms / privacy policy.

**Example 2**
You can create a colored panel bar. Inside this, you can insert a three columns from the Columns menu item. In the left column you can put your business name and address. In the center column, you can put a contact form. In the right column you can put links to important items, such as your menu items, your top store products, links to important websites, etc.
