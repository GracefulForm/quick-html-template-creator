# Footers
The bottom of your website is a common place to put important information.

<div style="text-align:left">
    <h2>Put It All Together</h2>
    <p>You can use the previous tools to build a footer.</p>

    <p><b>Example 1</b></p>
<p><p>You can use a colored panel bar found in the Color Sections menu and add your copyright, business name, and links to your terms / privacy policy.</p>

    <p><b>Example 2</b></p>
<p>You can create a colored panel bar. Inside this, you can insert a three columns from the Columns menu item. In the left column you can put your business name and address. In the center column, you can put a contact form. In the right column you can put links to important items, such as your menu items, your top store products, links to important websites, etc.</p>

<p>See what you can come up with!</p>
</div>
